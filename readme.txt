The picstoria app, also called Space Explorer
Admits three types of users which are:
-Simple users: Can only browse, search, view comments and share the app through social app
-Registered users: Can comment and tag pictures
-Admin staff: Can update the database by adding new pictures new administrator, they can also unpublish or publish a picture and all of its comments and tags or just unpublish a specific comment
the default account for the admin staff is: Username:"admin" Password:"admin"
